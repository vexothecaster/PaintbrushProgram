The Paintbrush - Gorilla Tag Exclusive Holdable

What is The Paintbrush?

The Paintbrush is an exclusive, custom holdable item in Gorilla Tag, designed specifically for special content creators within the community. This vibrant, colorful tool allows influencers to add a unique flair to their gameplay, stream, or video content.

Why is it Special?

Unlike other holdables in the game, the Paintbrush is only available to a select group of content creators. It symbolizes creativity, community engagement, and influence within the Gorilla Tag universe. Content creators who wield the Paintbrush are able to share their artistic and fun side, making their streams and videos stand out from the crowd.

Exclusive for Content Creators

This item is strictly for content creators who have earned the privilege to represent their creativity through this exclusive item.
